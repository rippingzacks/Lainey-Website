About Lainey

A realtor for the local family, not just the next transaction.

Lainey Sutika is a Lakewood Ranch-based Realtor® who works with families who are putting down roots — not flipping a house, but building the one they'll actually raise their kids in. That focus shapes everything about how she works.

Her specialty is new construction. It's a different process than buying a resale, with its own vocabulary, its own timeline, and its own ways for a family to get taken advantage of if no one is in their corner. Lainey sits on the buyer's side of that table: reviewing builder contracts line by line, sitting in on design center appointments so families don't get pressured into upgrades they don't need, and tracking construction milestones so nothing slips through the cracks between groundbreaking and the final walkthrough.

She's local — she knows which Lakewood Ranch villages fit a young family, which fit a couple looking to downsize, and which builders are currently delivering on schedule. Families work with her once and call her again for the next move, the in-laws' move, and the referral to a friend.

The New-Build Process

A roadmap most builders won't hand you.

Buying new construction means working directly with a builder whose sales team represents the builder — not you. Lainey walks alongside you at every stage below, at no direct cost in most cases, since the builder pays her commission.

  1. Phase 01

    Discovery

    Talk through budget, must-haves, school zones, and commute — then match it to the right Lakewood Ranch villages and builders.

  2. Phase 02

    Homesite Selection

    Tour active communities and reserve the right lot — orientation, elevation, and future neighbors all factor in.

  3. Phase 03

    Contract & Financing

    Review the builder's purchase agreement line by line and connect you with lenders who understand new-construction timelines.

  4. Phase 04

    Design Center

    Sit in on structural and finish selections so choices are about the home you want — not the upgrade with the best margin.

  5. Phase 05

    Construction Milestones

    Track framing, pre-drywall, and pre-closing inspections on your behalf, and flag issues before they're buried behind drywall.

  6. Phase 06

    Closing Day

    Final walkthrough, punch list, and keys in hand — with a Realtor® who was there from the first conversation.

Why It Matters

New construction isn't the same game as resale.

The builder's agent works for the builder

The friendly face at the model home is representing the seller. Lainey represents you, and reviews every selection and change order with your interests in mind.

Usually no added cost to you

In most new-construction transactions, the builder pays the buyer's agent commission — so having Lainey in your corner typically costs you nothing extra.

Local knowledge of Lakewood Ranch

Which villages are still releasing homesites, which builders are backed up, and which floor plans hold value — that's daily knowledge, not a quick search.

Someone tracking the timeline

New builds slip. Lainey stays on top of construction milestones and communicates delays before they become surprises at closing.
